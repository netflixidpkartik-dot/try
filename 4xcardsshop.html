Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

What are virtual prepaid cards?+
Virtual prepaid cards are digital payment cards with a card number, CVV and expiry date. They work exactly like physical cards for online purchases, subscriptions, and international transactions without requiring a bank account.
How long does card delivery take?+
Once your cryptocurrency payment is confirmed on the blockchain (typically 10–30 minutes), your card details are delivered instantly to your account dashboard under "My Purchases".
Which cryptocurrencies are accepted?+
We accept USDT (BEP-20 and TRC-20), Bitcoin (BTC), Litecoin (LTC), TRON (TRX), and TON. USDT on BEP-20 or TRC-20 is recommended for fastest and cheapest transactions.
Where can I use these cards?+
Our cards are accepted at all major online merchants, streaming platforms, e-commerce sites, travel bookings, and any platform that supports VISA, Mastercard, Amex or Discover internationally.
What does the card "limit" mean?+
The card limit is the maximum spendable balance loaded on the card. It is separate from the purchase price — a $10 card might have a $60–70 spendable limit, giving you excellent value.
Is my payment and data secure?+
Absolutely. All transactions use AES-256 encryption. We do not store raw payment details. Your card data is secured end-to-end and only accessible from your authenticated account.
Can I request a refund?+
Due to the digital nature of virtual cards, all sales are final once the card has been delivered. If you experience a technical issue or non-delivery after payment, contact our support team immediately.
Do I need to verify my identity (KYC)?+
No KYC is required for standard purchases. Simply create an account with your email, complete your crypto payment, and receive your card. High-volume orders may require additional verification.
What if my payment is not verified?+
Payment verification typically takes 10–30 minutes depending on blockchain congestion. If your payment is not confirmed after 60 minutes, contact support with your transaction hash and order ID.
How do I contact support?+
You can reach us via email at support@4xcardsshop.com or through our Telegram channel @4xCardSupport. Our team responds within 1–4 hours on all business days, 24/7 for urgent issues.
